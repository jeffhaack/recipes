Ingredients
-----------
* 1/3 cup olive oil
* tempeh
* 1/2 cup chopped onion
* 2 cloves garlic
* 1/2 cup bell pepper
* 1 cup pineapple
* 1 cup coconut milk
* 1/2 cup pure water
* 2 tsp organic curry powder
* 1 lime leaf
* 2" long lemongrass

Steps
-----
Cook Tempe, 2 minutes
Add onion and garlic, 3 minutes
Add everything else
Bring to a boil then reduce heat and simmer 3 minutes
garnish with basil
